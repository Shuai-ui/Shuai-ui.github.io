Key Findings

  • Danceability and Energy: These features are highly correlated with song popularity, suggesting that more danceable and energetic songs tend to be more popular.
  • Genre Specific Trends: Different music genres exhibit unique characteristics that influence their popularity. For instance, K-pop shows distinct patterns compared to Chill and Pop genres.
